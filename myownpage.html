MEMES

A look into the culture of today.

This is (not) Fine

This meme, often used to denote someones feelings about a particular situation, is funny becasue as people we tend to pretend that everything is okay, even when it obviously isnt fine at all.

What the??

This meme (best used as a reaction to a comment) is funny because the reaction of a 5 year old is as expressive and honest as one can get. Something that in an adult may be hard to express. Therefore we can rely on this image.

Pandemic

Day 18 of lockdown seems to be a little naive having been already 2 years into the pandemic. That's fine, since this image reflected an emotional state of back then. If 18 days does that to a person, imagine what 2 years did to the whole world.

Conclusion?

Memes, above all, are funny.
